Title: The Stars Are Legion

Author: Kameron Hurley

Category: Science

Language: English

Description:

The Stars Are Legion. On the edge of the universe, a mass of decaying world-ships known as the Legion is traveling in the seams between the stars. For generations, a war for control of the Legion has been waged, with no clear resolution. As worlds continue to die, a desperate plan is put into motion.
