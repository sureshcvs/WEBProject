Title: Love Story Nichole Nordeman

Author: Nichole Nordeman

Category: Love Stories

Language: English

Description:

Love Story is an exquisite narrative that exposes the emotional and human underside of major biblical events, including Adam and Eve's dramatic fall in the Garden of Eden, Sarah and Abraham's struggle to have a child, Mary's surprise at being pregnant with Jesus, Paul's trauma on the road to Damascus, and concludes with a triumphant picture of the second coming of Christ.
