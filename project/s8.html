Elysium Fire. Elysium Fire is a 2018 hard science fiction novel by Welsh author Alastair Reynolds. It is a direct sequel to Aurora Rising, taking place in the Revelation Space universe. Aurora Rising and Elysium Fire comprise the Prefect Dreyfus Emergency series.
